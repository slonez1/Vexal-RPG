# Lore and Knowledge

## Purpose
This file serves as the central repository for world-building content, including history, mythology, locations, and key characters.

---

## World Overview

### Geography and Regions
- **The Sunlit Kingdoms**: A realm of diverse landscapes where magic and martial prowess coexist.
- **Major Biomes**: Forests, mountains, deserts, swamps, and coastal regions.
- **Political Structure**: Multiple kingdoms and city-states, some allied, some in conflict.

### History
- **The Age of Ascension**: When mortals first learned to wield magic.
- **The Shadow Wars**: A conflict between light and dark forces that shaped the current world.
